Key Features

  • Rest assured of high data privacy - data lives only locally on your device (can be optionally synced to iCloud)
  • Add entries to 22 provided gratitude categories as you grow your collection of things to be grateful for, and add custom categories of your own
  • Export your data as CSV
  • Add, bookmark, edit, delete, or reorder all entries
  • Add, bookmark, edit, delete, or reorder custom categories, and bookmark or reorder system categories
  • View a heatmap of your journaling habit and a pie chart of your most used categories
  • Choose from 3 header fonts, 3 body fonts, and 2 color schemes for a personalized journaling experience
  • Use it on iPhone (voice input support), iPad (voice input and Pencil support), and macOS (select keyboard shortcuts)
  • Use accessibility features (reduced motion, dynamic text sizing, good voiceover coverage for the visually impaired)
  • View in 3 languages1
  • Journal in any language you want regardless of localization support - a boon for language learners
