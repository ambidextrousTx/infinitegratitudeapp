Privacy Policy for Infinite Gratitude

  • This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information of its users.
  • Journal entries are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud if enabled. Your data is encrypted as per standard Apple practices.
  • The developer does not have access to your data.
  • No analytics or tracking are used in this app. The graphical insights in the app are calculated locally on-device and on-demand.
